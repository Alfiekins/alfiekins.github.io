One Samboan


Samboan is one of the emerging towns in the island of Cebu, ranking 6th in the Philippines under the competitive index of LGUs as of 2017. Samboan is also a recipient of the National Seal of Good Governance in 2016 and 2017, and the Child Friendly municipality award in 2016.
